Serivise

Tubaha serivise dukurikije ubwoko bw'ibintu mwabuze. Mwakanda kuri serivise mukeneye.

Gushaka Ibyatakaye

Shakisha ibintu bifiteho imyirondoro yawe cyangwa itariho ariko wibuka ince wabitayemo. byaba ari irangamuntu, pasiporo, ibikapu, ibikoresho by'ikoranabuhanga. Ibitariho imyirondoro nk'ibikapu cyangwa ibikoresho by'ikoranabuhanga, wabishakisha ukurikije mafoto cyangwa ahantu waba wabitaye.

Kurangisha Ibyatoraguwe

Imyenda, ibikapu by'ubwoko bwose, n'ibindi bikoresho byo gutwara ibintu nk'ikofi cyangwa amashakoshi.

Gutangiza Konte Ntakiguzi

Tangira konte yawe nta kiguzi na kimwe. Konte izagufasha gutangaza ikintu wabuze kugira ngo igihe cyabonetse uzabashe kumenyeshwa ako kanya.
